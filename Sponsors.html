Sponsorship

We need your help to support our NSBE chapter.

Sponorsing the Virginia Tech Chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers is an amazing opportunity for your organization or company. Our chapter produces world class engineers and entreprenuers, and your donation can go a long way to providing scholarships, community outreach activity support, and more. We use the funding provided by our corporate sponors to sponor trips for youth interested in STEM, funding for conferences, chapter meetings, and more. If you are interested in sponoring our NSBE chapter please contact us, at vt.nsbe.president2@gmail.com.
