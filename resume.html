Resume Book Information

The Virginia Tech Chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers offers our members the opportunity to have their resumes entered into a chapter resume book. We have numerous inquires for job opportunities to our chapter looking for memebers like yourself to fill those roles. If you are interested in having your resume featured in our covetted resume book, please fill out the form below and email the form and your resume to vt.nsbe.president2@gmail.com. You must have paid chapter dues in order to have your resume featured in the resume book.
